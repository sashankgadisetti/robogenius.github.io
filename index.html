Why Us?

Our goal here at RxAcademy is to help your child grow a passion for robotics and the field of computer science. Our curriculum is designed introduce you to computer science, and help you grow your knowledge and skillset to help you in high school, college and beyond. Our boot-camps provide a quick, yet in-depth introduction for your child, helping spark their interest and curiosity. We are a passionate group of computer science undergrad students who have years of teaching experience and computer science knowledge. We hope to see you soon!
